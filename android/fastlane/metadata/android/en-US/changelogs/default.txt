v2.15
- Encrypt a whole folder (structure preserved) or multiple files at once, not just a single file; new Compress toggle in advanced settings.
- Cross-platform passwords: non-ASCII passwords are normalized (Unicode NFC), so a password typed on another OS decrypts here; existing volumes still open.
- Smaller downloads: per-architecture APKs (arm64-v8a, armeabi-v7a, x86, x86_64) alongside the universal APK.
- Privacy: optional screenshot protection — blocks screenshots and screen recording and hides the app in recent apps; on by default, toggle on the main screen.
